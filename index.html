Minecraft Emoji CSS

Want to include Minecraft emoji in your HTML? Just include the (minified) stylesheet below, then add <i> tags to insert emoji. That's it!
✈ Got flash enabled? Click the emoji code and it will be copied to your clipboard. 

<link href="//emoji.minecraftly.com/emoji.css" rel="stylesheet">

<i class="mc mc-some-emoji"></i>
